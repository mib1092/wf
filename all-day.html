PLAN YOUR JULY 4th

ALL DAY

SEE FULL EVENT SCHEDULE FULL EVENT CALENDAR
  • 8:00AM

    Cup of Joe

    Paramour Coffee

    Start your morning off right with a cup of the best coffee around from Paramour Artisan Coffee.

    Insider tip: Their maple bacon scones are out of this world!

  • 8:30AM

    Breakfast Time

    The Friendship House

    Grab breakfast at The Friendship House. Don’t forget to try their famous breakfast bierocks!

  • 10:00AM

    Classic Car Show

    Main Street

    Take a walk down memory lane at the 42nd Annual Walter P. Chrysler Car Show on Main Street. With an average of over 100 entries, there’s something for everyone. Hours: 9:00am to 3:30pm.

  • 11:00AM

    Antique Tractor Show

    Rec Complex

    Stop by the 31st Annual Kaw Valley Antique Engine, Truck and Tractor Club Show to see dozens of unique farm equipment from all over the U.S. Hours: 9:00am to 3:30pm.

  • 11:30AM

    Lunch Time?

    6th and Lincoln

    Beat the crowd and head over to the First United Methodist Church for their annual pork sandwich and homemade pie meal. This 100 year old tradition is a local favorite you won’t want to miss! Hours: 11:00am to 8:00pm.

  • 1:00PM

    Sweet Tooth

    City Park

    There’s always room for dessert, right? Stop by Wamego Historical Society ice cream and cake social for a sweet treat.

  • 1:30PM

    History

    Columbian Theatre

    If you’re a fan of amazing music, head to The Columbian Theatre to hear Wamego’s Community Band! While you’re there, make sure you tour the historic theatre and view the rare paintings from the 1893 Chicago World's Fair. These rare pieces will take your breath away.

  • 2:00PM

    Carnival Rides

    City Park

    Head over to the carnival where we have rides and thrill levels for all ages. From the ferris wheel and tilt-a-whirl, to your favorite carnival games and snacks, our carnival has something for everyone. And who knows; you might just walk away with a prize! Hours: 1:00pm to 10:00pm.

  • 3:00PM

    Visit OZ

    OZ Museum

    Experience the World of OZ at the famous OZ Museum downtown. It showcases over over 2,000 artifacts dating from 1900 to present day. You will find detailed historical facts about the movie and actors, OZ memorabilia, and so much more!

  • 4:00PM

    Patriotic Tunes

    6th and Lincoln

    Cool off inside the First United Methodist Church while listening to the amazing Patriotic Vocal Concert.

  • 5:00PM

    TOTOlly OZsome Food

    Toto’s Tacoz

    A trip to Wamego isn’t complete without a meal from Toto’s Tacoz. The Yellow Brick Burrito, Dorothy's Quesadilla and Auntie Em’s Nachos are some of their famous dishes, but no matter what you get you won’t be disappointed. All of their food is OZ-some!

  • 5:30PM

    Front Row Seat

    Main Street

    The Fourth of July parade doesn’t kick off until 6:00 PM, but make your way to Main Street early to save your seat. It fills up fast!

    Insider tip: Try and grab a seat on the West side of the street so you’re in the shade. :)

  • 6:00PM

    Parade

    Main Street

    If you’re coming to the Wamego Fourth of July celebration, you can’t miss their parade! This year marks 145 years of classic cars, area floats and incredible live music. Plus, who doesn’t like a little free candy?

  • 8:00PM

    Grab Your Spot

    Rec Complex

    Make your way to the fireworks viewing area to grab a seat and enjoy the 1st Infantry Division Band pre-show concert while you wait. Concessions are still open, so grab a funnel cake or slushie on your way to enjoy while you walk!

  • 10:00PM

    It’s Showtime

    Rec Complex

    Sit back, relax and enjoy Kansas’ #1 Fireworks show. This 33 minute and 29 second performance will not leave you disappointed.

  • 10:30PM

    More Tunes

    Rec Complex

    Avoid the traffic. Check out the post-show entertainment provided by the 1st Infantry Division Band.

    Insider tip: Allow the traffic to thin out a little bit before you leave for a speedy ride home.
